History

Keanu Reeves is an Asian Canadian actor born in Beirut Lebanon on September 2, 1964. His name means "cool breeze over the mountains" in Hawaiian which is his fathers native language. He was born to Patricia Taylor and Samuel Nowlin Reeves Jr. His mom stemmed from Essex, England and His father originating from the small island Kauai. Shortly after Keanu was born his dad left and Reeves hasn't seen his father since he was only 13.

After his father left his family moved to Sydney, Australia. He didn't live in Sydney for long and soon after moving there his family moved to New York City. In New York was where his mom Patricia Taylor met her next husband Paul Aaron. Keanu's mom and Paul were in the same industry and that is how they met.

Shortly after his mom married Paul they moved to Toronto, Canada and got divorced shortly after. Keanu first started acting at the age of nine and was a part of the theatre production of the play Damn Yankees. His step father Aaron stayed very close to Keanu and helped him get a job at the Hedgerow Theater.

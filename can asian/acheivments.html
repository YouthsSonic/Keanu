Acheivements

Keanu Reeves has had many huge achievements and here are some of them. Keanu started his career doing plays, advertisments and Tv shows. He eventually made his debut in movies in 1985 in the short film One Step Away. Some of the largest movies Keanu has been in are The Matrix, John Wick, Cyberpunk and Bill and Teds Bogus Journey. Even though Keanu hasn't won an Oscar yet movies he has been in like The Matrix has won Academy Awards for best filming and editing, best sound editing, best sound and best visual effects. Throughout Keanu's career he has won many awards like MTV movie award for best fight, Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie: Liplock and MTV Movie Award for Best Male Performance. Keanu is also known for being extremely charitable and kind.
